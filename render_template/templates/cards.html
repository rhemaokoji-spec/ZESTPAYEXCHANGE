FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I get a ZestPay card?

Getting a ZestPay card is simple! Sign up for an account, complete the verification process, and you'll be able to request your virtual or physical card directly from the app. Virtual cards are issued instantly, while physical cards are delivered within 5-7 business days.

What are the fees associated with ZestPay cards?

ZestPay offers transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Virtual cards have a minimal issuance fee, while physical cards have a one-time delivery fee. Transaction fees are competitive and clearly displayed before you confirm any payment. ATM withdrawals and international transactions may have additional fees.

Is my money safe with ZestPay?

Absolutely! We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and advanced fraud detection systems. Your funds are held in secured accounts with reputable financial institutions, and we're fully licensed and regulated by financial authorities.

Can I use ZestPay cards internationally?

Yes! ZestPay cards are accepted worldwide wherever Visa and Mastercard are accepted. You can use them for online purchases, in-store payments, and ATM withdrawals globally. We automatically handle currency conversion at competitive exchange rates.

How do I load funds onto my ZestPay card?

You can load funds through multiple methods: bank transfer, debit/credit card, cryptocurrency exchange, or from other ZestPay users. All deposits are processed instantly, and you'll receive a notification as soon as your funds are available.

What currencies are supported by ZestPay cards?

ZestPay supports multiple major currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, GHS, KES, ZAR, and more. You can hold balances in multiple currencies and switch between them seamlessly with our real-time exchange rates.

How do I report a lost or stolen card?

If your card is lost or stolen, you can immediately freeze it through the ZestPay app or by contacting our support team. We'll issue a replacement card right away, and you won't be liable for any unauthorized transactions made after you report the loss.

Are there spending limits on ZestPay cards?

Yes, there are default spending limits for security purposes, but these can be adjusted in your account settings. Virtual cards typically have lower limits than physical cards. Premium account holders enjoy higher limits and more flexible terms.

How does ZestPay protect against fraud?

We employ multiple layers of security including real-time transaction monitoring, AI-powered fraud detection, location-based verification, and instant transaction alerts. You can also set up custom spending alerts and temporary card freezes for additional security.

Can I use my ZestPay card with mobile payment services?

Absolutely! ZestPay cards are fully compatible with Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, and other major mobile payment services. Simply add your card to your preferred wallet app and enjoy contactless payments with your phone or smartwatch.

What happens if a transaction is disputed?

If you need to dispute a transaction, simply contact our support team within 30 days of the transaction date. We'll investigate the dispute on your behalf and work with the merchant to resolve the issue. We follow all standard chargeback procedures and consumer protection regulations.

How do I close my ZestPay account?

If you need to close your account, ensure all pending transactions are complete and your balance is zero. You can initiate the closure process through the app or by contacting customer support. We'll process your request within 5 business days and provide confirmation once completed.

Does ZestPay offer business accounts?

Yes! ZestPay Business offers specialized features for entrepreneurs and companies including multi-user accounts, expense management tools, corporate cards, accounting integrations, and preferential rates for high-volume transactions. Contact our business team for a customized solution.
